Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker an "idle" game. The goal is to, well, click on a cookie. That might seem simple but over time you start to get cursors, then enslaved grandmas, then cookie mines, then a cookie universe, until finally cookie nirvana. It's pretty good at helping to pass the time and if you really want to progress you can just wait.
